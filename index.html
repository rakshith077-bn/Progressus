A House Expense Manager

Manage your house expenses using this simple tool and get real time analytics, crystal clear pictorial, solution oriented representation of your expenses and make Data Driven Decisions. The difference between making decisions based on instict and data makes it all into a power house. The simplistic design and the power of Data makes it a powerful tool to handle your expenses.



A power packed tool to maketh your home expenses perfect

Eloquent yet Simple

Structured in a universal way to match substantially all home expenses. From naming your own expense to the number of expenses, you can make it possible to record all expenses. Even if we dont cover it, you add it.

Insights

Get real time analytics and solution oriented representation of your expenses and make the most of these representations to conclude to where you want to spend more or where you could cut down on your expenses and do much more.

Data Driven Decisions

Make decisions with your base as data, not instinct. Learn, Understand and Improve your expenses based on the real time analytics from Google Data Studio. Compare your Actual expenses v/s your Planned Expenses and get top notch analytics which involves you to do much more.

Universal

You have the option to select the total number of people in your family and get individual analysis as well as the total expenses of the family all in the analytics page

Just how powerful?


Plan your Spendings

Schedule your spendings by planning and visualizing it in your own mind. VISUALISE, IMPROVISE and ADAPT

Application Area

Substantially to all household expenses except business and other commercial expense sheets. Small businesses like small-scale restaurants which could replace all unwanted entities to all the required ones.

Analytics

Now to the main focus of this tool. Get visuasations and real time, live analytics from Google Data Studio and make decsions by comparing your Actual v/s Planned expenses and much more. Know more about the analytics part.

What makes this the right fit?

Know the power of Data. Data Driven Decision is the process of making decisions based on data, to bring about a well rounded efficient decsions with the base as Data and not instinct.
