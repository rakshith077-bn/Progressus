Progressus

A Houe Expense Manager Progressus is a power packed tool made in order to help your reach your financialy expectations by analysing and making smarter decisions based on real data given by you.

Enter your data in the Analytics page and keep your consistency. Within a month you can clearly understand and make decsions based on numbers rather than instinct.

Problem Statement

The whole sense of bringing Progressus into life was to figure out a way to find a better if not the best way to make daily and your life's expenses easier to handle.

With this in mind we also got into a conclusion of including how to make this project into a solution oriented personified project which would make a differnece in everyday society and if used on a daily basis could make a huge differnece on the people struggling to find it hard to save up some money at the end of the month.

Proposed solution

Progressus was a long term goal of ours. Looking back into our daily life, our parents maintained a diary or a book where they wrote down all the expenses be it little or big. We never understood why this was done. Later in our life's we found just how important and the role played by the data recorded by our parents could make.

With this in mind, we started to look deep into and asked our parents how they helped themselves to make decsions based on the data they collect, and how did it xhange their way of conducting expenses?

The answer was rather intresting, looking back at the previous month's expenses our parents found that the household bills like the power bill was through the roof. They wanted to know what was the problem even though they were using less power. By looking deeper, they found that their fridge which was rather old was consuming more power and used to regularly short-circut the whole home's electricity.

In order to compensate on buying a new fridge they cut down the Entertainment and the resturant bills for over three months in order to pay the EMI bills.

Looking at how the collected data made such a huge difference, we wanted to bring this power of analysis into life. At the same time we were proposed to do the Mini-DBMS Porject. This showed us that if we could breathe life into Progressus we could make a huge differnece on how we could bring this power of analysis to the others.
